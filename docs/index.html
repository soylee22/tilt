How it works

  1. At each month-end close, compute every ETF's 12-month total return.
  2. From the factor basket, pick the single ETF with the highest 12m return.
  3. From the sector basket, pick the single ETF with the highest 12m return.
  4. Hold both 50/50 for the next month. If a pick is unchanged from last month, no trade.
  5. Repeat. No discretion, no opinion, no Stage 2 gates, no composite ranker. Pure relative momentum.

Lineage: Antonacci Dual Momentum + Faber Tactical Asset Allocation + MarketFighter Substack. Backtested 2019-04 to 2026-05 over the 20-ETF universe above: 23.6% CAGR, Sharpe 1.02, max DD -31%. Forward returns will be lower. Survivorship bias: zero (this is index-tracking ETFs not individual stocks).

Disclaimers. Educational only. Past performance does not predict future returns. The backtest assumes a Euro/GBP-denominated investor and ignores UK CGT. Slippage modelled at 5 bps round-trip.
